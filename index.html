Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Your premium subscription will be restored automatically if you have an active subscription linked to your Apple ID or Google account.

How do I host a multiplayer game?

Premium subscribers can create a multiplayer room from the home screen. Once created, share the room code or QR code with your group. Players join by entering the code or scanning it.

Can I play for free?

Yes. Free players can join any multiplayer game and play one solo game per calendar month. Premium unlocks unlimited solo play and the ability to host.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store. On iOS, go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions. On Android, open the Google Play Store → Subscriptions.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the Settings screen within the app. This will remove your account data, game history, and leaderboard entries.

Is my money real?

No. All scores, winnings, rankings, and in-game values are virtual gameplay values only. Nothing is redeemable for real money.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Introduction

Quiz Millions ("the app") is developed and operated by Nuno Ricardo Moura Fontes. This policy describes what information the app collects, how it is used, and what choices you have.

What We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may store your account identifier, display name, avatar preference, subscription status, game results, leaderboard statistics, and multiplayer room participation data. If you sign in with Apple, Google, or email, your authentication credentials are handled by the respective provider.

Gameplay Data

The app records gameplay data including completed games, answers given, scores, rankings, and question-level performance metrics. This data is used to run the game, maintain leaderboards, prevent cheating, and improve question quality over time.

Multiplayer Data

When you join a multiplayer room, your display name, avatar, answers, score, and standings are visible to other players in that room as part of the game experience.

Device Permissions

The app requests access to your device's camera only when you choose to scan a QR code to join a multiplayer game. The camera is used solely to detect a join code in the live preview. Camera images and video are never stored, transmitted, shared with third parties, or used for any other purpose, and the app does not access the camera in the background.

Subscriptions

If you purchase a premium subscription, the transaction is processed by Apple or Google through their respective app store. We receive confirmation of your subscription status to unlock premium features but do not have access to your payment details.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party infrastructure to provide its core features. Authentication, database storage, and real-time multiplayer are powered by Supabase. Subscription entitlements are managed by RevenueCat. Sign-in with Apple is handled by Apple, and Sign-in with Google is handled by Google. In-app purchases are processed by the Apple App Store on iOS and by Google Play Billing on Android. These providers process data on our behalf strictly to deliver app functionality. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on cloud infrastructure operated by our service providers. Data in transit between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

You can stop using the app at any time. If you use the account deletion feature in the app, your account-related data will be removed, subject to any technical, fraud-prevention, billing, or legal retention requirements.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. Continued use of the app after changes take effect means the updated policy applies to you.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, email us.
